Our Mission

Our main goal is to bring a smile on every struggling face

We are network of volunteers who volunteer to deliver essentials like food, clothes, footwears, books, toys, donations etc to all the NGO's registered under the Government of India in various cities. Our volunteers work on locality basis and deliver the essentials from the local city residences to the NGO's in that locality in a way reducing the need for normal people to travel to NGO's.
We also offer the facility to NGO's for calling out to the city population for any events like tree plantations, beach cleaning, animal protection etc on given days.

Our mission is to bring a smile on every struggling face. In this way, we bring happiness to the people in need, satisfaction to the people who donate essentials and pride to volunteers for serving the society.
